Ceremony in Brazil X

We will be having a wedding ceremony in Shridhar’s home city following Indian traditions and would be delighted if you would please join us!

Location: São José dos Campos - SP
Date: Saturday, February 27th, 2016.

Visa to Brazil

US citizens need to obtain a visa from a Brazilian consulate. The specific consulate you will need to obtain your visa from depends on your state of residence. The jurisdiction list can be found in this site. The visa can be obtained in person or via a registered courier service. If you will be obtaining your visa from the Los Angeles Consulate using a courier, we suggest United PVS. If you will be obtaining a visa from the San Francisco Consulate using a courier, we suggest TDS.

Airport

We recommend you fly into the São Paulo-Guarulhos International airport (GRU) which is about one hour from São José dos Campos. Other nearby airports include the São José dos Campos airport (SJK) and the São Paulo-Congonhas airport (CGH).

Lodging

We will be reserving a block of rooms at the Ibis Hotel (Dutra), but please feel free to stay in any hotel in São José dos Campos, transportation within the city is close and inexpensive. We will arrange transportation for you from your choice hotel to the venue.

Tourist spots

    Nearby locations that can be easily reached:
  • Rio de Janeiro, at a mere 5 hour bus ride away, is one of the prettiest cities in the world, and the most visited in the Southern Hemisphere. Rio is home to the iconic beaches of Ipanema and Copacabana, as well as the Christ the Redeemer statue, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
  • São Paulo, the second largest metropolis in the world, is only an hour drive away. São Paulo is a true melting pot being home to the largest Italian, Arab and Japanese diasporas. The diversity of cultures helped earn São Paulo the title of “World Gastronomy Capital” in 1997, and Alex Atala’s restaurant D.O.M., famed for serving indigenous flora from the Amazon, was named one of the top ten restaurants in the world.
  • Aparecida**, the second largest church is easily accessible in a day trip.
    • Other places of interest in Brazil:
  • Amazon rain forest**
  • Iguazu falls and Itaipu Power Dam**
  • Pantanal**
