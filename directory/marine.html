Marine Covers & Enclosures

In Canvas offers a large selection of Marine fabrics and vinyl. These materials are marine use specific and withstand the rough elements of ocean and weather. They are UV stable, water repellent, mildew resistant, and stain guarded.

Our frames are custom made in house to meet your specific design and height requirements. Frames are stainless steel or aluminum depending on enclosure type and size.
